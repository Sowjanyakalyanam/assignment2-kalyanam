My favorite movie is Sita Ramam

A period piece set against the backdrop of war in the 1960s is Sita Ramam. A fresh and intriguing notion is a love story set against a background of conflict. The film's songs are at the top of the music charts, and the trailer is excellent. It has been a long time since there was music this pure and timeless as Sita Ramam.
